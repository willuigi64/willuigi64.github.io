About Hollow

Hollow is the official theme song of Final Fantasy VII Remake. Composed by Nobuo Uematsu, lyrics by Kazushige Nojima and performed by Yosh Morita (Survive Said the Prophet). This track is heard once throughout the 30+ hour playtime of Final Fantasy VII Remake at the end credits. The song is from the main character, Cloud Strife's perspective. There is an instrumental arrangement by Yasunori Nishiki titled Hollow Skies that plays in areas of the Sector 5 Slums earlier in the game.

About Survive Said The Prophet

SSTP are a Japanese rock band formed in 2011. Currently signed to Sony Music Records, they're most recognized for creating anime opening songs.

Yosh Morita

The vocalist of SSTP, Yosh was chosen to sing Hollow's lyrics by Uematsu. Uematsu is a huge rocker, having been in his own rock bands.

About Nobuo Uematsu

Legendary composer known for his work at Square Enix (Previously known as Square, or SquareSoft) on the Final Fantasy series. Having worked of FF, Chrono Trigger, Lost Odyssey, and many more. He went independent in 2006 with his own label, continued to work with Square Enix and other companies to create more legendary music, like Hollow.

About Kazushige Nojima

Another legend from Square, Nojima was the main writer for many critically acclaimed Final Fantasy titles like 7, 8, 10, 15, and 7 Remake. His history with FF7 and having written its characters make him the perfect writer for Hollow's lyrics.
